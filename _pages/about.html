Based in the Washington D.C. area, Sarah, Valerie, and Lindsay are data scientists for Booz Allen Hamilton.

Sarah Cameron is a data scientist with a background in both Engineering and Data Analytics. Her interests are in time series forecasting and predictive modeling. Also a teaching assistant at Boston University, Sarah has an active interest in mentoring aspiring data scientists.


Valerie Schnapp

Valerie has been on a span of projects, and is currently working as part of the data science team where she is building out different consumption time series models and working on an optimization effort.


Lindsay Truong

Lindsay Truong is a University of Virginia alumna with a background in Economics. Experienced in Python and R, she's supported multiple predictive modeling efforts. Her main interests lie in natural language processing and demand forecasting.
